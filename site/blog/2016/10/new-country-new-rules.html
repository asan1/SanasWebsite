New Country New Rules!

"October 29th, 2016"

As the winter winds were changing into spring breezes I was busy packing up for the trip back home. About six months away from Pakistan and a lot has happened during this time. i can happily say that ive made most of my stay in Australia this time. between Maryam's school, Ibrahim's day care, regular library story time visits, tutoring math students and finishing up my book I have been one busy mom!

Settling in has been a lot of fun though. Meeting up with the family, calling up hitherto ignored family memebers and dealing with the house overridden with dust balls.

Meanwhile the saga of the cleaning ladies continues and happy to say my dear old aunty is back after a near invasion from a vindictive and higly schmeing cleaning lady. No i am not exagerrating! but all this is part and package of life at Pakistan so stay tuned for more drama!!!
